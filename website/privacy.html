1. Information We Collect

Data Collection Summary

We collect different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Services to you.

1.1 Personal Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • For Users/Customers: Full name, email address, phone number, delivery address, payment information, and profile picture (optional).
  • For Dealers/Businesses: Business name, GST number, business address, contact person details, business registration documents, bank account details for payments, and authorized signatory information.
  • Contact Information: Email addresses, phone numbers, and mailing addresses.
  • Identity Information: For dealer verification, we may collect government-issued ID details as required by law.

1.2 Transaction Information

When you use our Services, we collect:

  • Order details (products purchased, quantities, prices)
  • Payment transaction details (excluding complete credit card numbers)
  • Delivery and shipping information
  • Return and refund history
  • Communication records with dealers or customers

1.3 Technical & Usage Information

Automatically collected information includes:

  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, browser type, mobile network information, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, search queries, time spent on the app, and clickstream data.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we may collect precise or approximate location data to provide location-based services.
  • Log Files: Server logs that include IP addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamps, referring/exit pages.

1.4 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you interact with our Services
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements
Type of Data Purpose of Collection Legal Basis
Personal Information Account creation, service delivery, communication Contractual necessity, legitimate interest
Transaction Data Order processing, payment, delivery Contractual necessity, legal obligation
Technical Data Service improvement, security, analytics Legitimate interest
Location Data Localized services, delivery tracking Consent, contractual necessity
Marketing Data Personalized offers, promotions Consent, legitimate interest

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Primary Uses

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • To process transactions and send related information
  • To verify dealer accounts and user identities
  • To facilitate communication between dealers and users
  • To send administrative messages, updates, and security alerts

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process orders and payments
  • Coordinate product deliveries
  • Provide customer support
  • Send order confirmations and updates

2.2 Communication

  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Send important service-related notices
  • Provide updates about your orders
  • Send promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Conduct surveys and gather feedback

2.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our Services
  • Develop new features and functionality
  • Personalize your experience
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Conduct research and analysis

2.4 Security & Compliance

  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • Protect against fraud and unauthorized transactions
  • Enforce our Terms and Conditions
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect the rights and safety of our users and the public

Important

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is used solely to provide and improve our services, and we only share it as described in this Privacy Policy or with your explicit consent.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 With Service Providers

We share information with third-party vendors who help us operate our business:

  • Payment Processors: To securely process transactions
  • Hosting Providers: For data storage and server management
  • Analytics Services: To understand how our Services are used
  • Marketing Partners: To send communications (with consent)
  • Delivery Partners: To coordinate product shipments

3.2 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information:

  • To comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To investigate potential violations of our Terms
  • To respond to legal requests from authorities
  • In connection with any merger, sale, or acquisition

3.3 Between Users and Dealers

To facilitate transactions, we share necessary information:

  • Dealers receive user contact and delivery information for order fulfillment
  • Users receive dealer business information for verification
  • Both parties can communicate through our secure messaging system

3.4 With Your Consent

We may share information with third parties when you give us explicit permission to do so.

4. Data Security

Our Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: Data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure Servers: Data stored on secure servers with firewall protection
  • Regular Audits: Security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee Training: Security awareness training for all employees

4.2 Payment Security

We use PCI-DSS compliant payment processors. Your payment information is encrypted and processed by secure third-party providers. We do not store complete credit card numbers on our servers.

4.3 Security Breach Procedures

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery
  • Take immediate steps to contain the breach
  • Investigate the cause and implement corrective measures
  • Comply with all legal reporting requirements

Your Role in Security

While we implement robust security measures, you also play a role in protecting your information. Please:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for your account
  • Enable two-factor authentication if available
  • Keep your login credentials confidential
  • Log out after using shared devices
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5.1 Retention Periods

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for 5 years after deactivation for legal and tax purposes
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax laws
  • Communication Records: Retained for 3 years for customer service purposes
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you opt-out or for 2 years after last engagement
  • Dealer Documents: Retained for 10 years for regulatory compliance

5.2 Data Deletion

Upon account deletion request, we will:

  • Anonymize or delete your personal data within 30 days
  • Retain necessary transaction records for legal compliance
  • Remove you from marketing communications immediately
  • Provide confirmation of data deletion

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Your Privacy Rights

As a Trade-Wala user, you have control over your personal information. You can exercise these rights by contacting us at privacy@trade-wala.com.

6.1 Access and Portability

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Right to Explanation: Understand how we use your data

6.2 Correction and Deletion

  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

6.3 Objection and Withdrawal

  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (for consent-based processing)
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications

6.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@trade-wala.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Services and hold certain information.

7.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the Services to function
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how our Services are used
  • Marketing Cookies: Track effectiveness of marketing campaigns

7.2 Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, if you limit the ability of websites to set cookies, you may worsen your overall user experience.

8. Children's Privacy

Age Restriction

Our Services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information. If you believe we might have any information from or about a child under 18, please contact us at privacy@trade-wala.com.

9.2 Your Continued Use

Your continued use of the Services after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes acceptance of those changes.
