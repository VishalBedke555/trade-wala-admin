Powerful Features

Trade-Wala provides a comprehensive solution for construction material management with dedicated panels for dealers and users.

Dealer Panel

Dealers can easily upload products, create categories, manage inventory, track orders, and analyze sales performance.

User Panel

Users can browse products by category, compare prices, place orders, track deliveries, and manage their purchase history.

Product Management

Organize construction materials into categories like cement, steel, aggregates, tools, and more with detailed specifications.

Order Tracking

Real-time order tracking from placement to delivery with status updates and estimated delivery times.

Analytics Dashboard

Dealers get access to sales analytics, inventory reports, and customer insights to grow their business.

Mobile App

Available for both Android and iOS devices with intuitive interface and offline capabilities.
