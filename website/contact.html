Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Trade-Wala

How do I become a verified dealer on Trade-Wala?

+

To become a verified dealer, visit our website and click on "Register as Dealer". You'll need to submit your business registration documents, GST certificate, address proof, and bank details. Our verification team will review your application within 3-5 business days. Once approved, you'll gain access to the dealer dashboard where you can start listing products.

What types of construction materials can I find on Trade-Wala?

+

Trade-Wala features a comprehensive range of construction materials including:

  • Cement & Aggregates (OPC, PPC, RMC, Sand, Aggregates)
  • Steel & Reinforcement (TMT Bars, Structural Steel, Wire Mesh)
  • Bricks, Blocks & Tiles (Clay Bricks, AAC Blocks, Floor Tiles, Wall Tiles)
  • Plumbing & Electrical Materials (Pipes, Fittings, Wires, Switches)
  • Tools & Safety Equipment (Power Tools, Hand Tools, Safety Gear)
  • Paints & Finishes (Interior, Exterior, Primers, Sealants)

How do I track my order?

+

Once your order is confirmed and shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email. You can track your order in real-time through the "My Orders" section in the Trade-Wala app or website. For dealer-to-dealer bulk orders, you'll also receive regular updates on dispatch and estimated delivery times through your dealer dashboard.

What is your return policy for defective materials?

+

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for damaged or incorrect items. Please contact our support team with your order number and photos/videos of the issue. Our quality team will verify the complaint and initiate the return process. Return shipping is free for damaged/incorrect items. For quality disputes, we may send a representative to inspect the materials at your site.

How are payments processed and secured?

+

Trade-Wala uses industry-leading payment gateways with 256-bit SSL encryption to ensure secure transactions. We support multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and wallet payments. For large dealer transactions, we also offer secure bank transfer options with payment protection. All payment information is encrypted and never stored on our servers.
