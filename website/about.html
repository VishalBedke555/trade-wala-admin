About Trade-Wala

Revolutionizing construction material management through technology, connecting dealers and users across India with a seamless, efficient platform.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Trade-Wala emerged from a simple idea: to simplify the complex world of construction material procurement and management.

Building the future of construction material trade

Trade-Wala Founding Team, 2020

From Vision to Reality

Trade-Wala was founded by a team of construction industry veterans and technology enthusiasts who recognized the challenges faced by both material dealers and construction professionals in sourcing and managing materials.

We noticed that the construction material industry was largely fragmented, with dealers struggling to reach customers and users facing difficulties in comparing prices, verifying quality, and ensuring timely delivery.

Our mission became clear: create a digital platform that bridges this gap, bringing transparency, efficiency, and convenience to construction material procurement across India.

Today, Trade-Wala serves thousands of dealers and construction professionals, facilitating millions of rupees in transactions monthly and continuously expanding our reach across the country.

Our Mission

To revolutionize construction material management by creating the most efficient, transparent, and user-friendly digital marketplace that empowers dealers to grow their business and enables users to make informed purchasing decisions with ease and confidence.

Our Vision

To become India's leading construction material management platform, recognized for transforming traditional procurement processes through innovation, technology, and exceptional user experience, ultimately contributing to the growth of India's construction industry.
