What Happens When You Delete Your Account:

  • Order History

    Your complete order history will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

  • Saved Addresses

    All your saved delivery addresses will be removed from our system.

  • Payment Methods

    Your saved payment methods will be deleted for security reasons.

  • Reviews & Ratings

    Your product reviews and dealer ratings will be anonymized.

Important Information

Before proceeding, please ensure:

  • You have no pending orders or refunds
  • You have saved any important information from your account
  • You understand this action is irreversible
  • You won't be able to recover your account data

Account deletion may take up to 30 days to complete from all our systems.

Final Confirmation

This Action Cannot Be Undone

Once you delete your account, there is no going back. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers. Please be certain before proceeding.

What Happens When You Delete Your Dealer Account:

  • Product Listings

    All your product listings will be removed from the platform immediately.

  • Sales History

    Your complete sales history and analytics data will be permanently deleted.

  • Pending Transactions

    Any pending payments or commissions will be settled before account deletion.

  • Customer Relationships

    Your customer data and communication history will be anonymized.

  • Business Rating

    Your dealer rating and reviews will be removed from the platform.

  • Business Information

    Your GST, business registration, and verification documents will be deleted.

Important Notice for Dealers

Deleting your dealer account will affect all users who have purchased from you. We recommend:

  • Fulfilling all pending orders before deletion
  • Notifying your regular customers
  • Settling all payment disputes
  • Downloading your sales reports for tax purposes
  • Resolving any customer complaints

Dealer account deletion may take up to 7 business days to complete all settlement processes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to delete my account?

+

User accounts are typically deleted within 30 days. Dealer accounts may take up to 7 business days to complete all settlement processes. You will receive email confirmation once the deletion is complete.

Can I recover my account after deletion?

+

No, account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, all your data is permanently removed from our servers. If you contact us within 24 hours of submitting the deletion request, we may be able to cancel the process.

What happens to my pending orders?

+

For user accounts, all pending orders must be completed or cancelled before deletion. For dealer accounts, you must fulfill all pending orders and resolve any disputes. The system will not allow account deletion with active pending orders.

Will my reviews be deleted?

+

User reviews will be anonymized (displayed as "Deleted User"). Dealer reviews will be removed entirely. This helps maintain the integrity of our review system while respecting your privacy.

Can I delete my account through the mobile app?

+

Yes, you can initiate account deletion through the app settings. However, for security reasons, you'll still need to verify your identity through OTP and confirm the deletion on a secure page like this one.
